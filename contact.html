Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services

How do I book a tour with Spangtik Adventure?

You can book a tour with us through our website by visiting the Tour Packages page and selecting "Book Now" on your preferred package. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via email or phone to make a booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for a full refund if cancellation is made 30 days or more before the tour start date. Cancellations made 15-29 days before will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 15 days before the tour start date are non-refundable.

Do you offer customized tour packages?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom tour packages tailored to your preferences, timeframe, and budget. Contact our team with your requirements, and we'll design a personalized itinerary just for you.

What should I pack for a trekking tour?

Essential items include appropriate clothing for varying temperatures, sturdy hiking boots, a quality backpack, sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, water bottle, personal medications, and a camera. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking based on your specific tour and the season.

Do I need any special permits for trekking in Northern Pakistan?

Yes, certain areas in Northern Pakistan require permits. When you book with us, we handle all necessary permits as part of our service, so you don't need to worry about this aspect.

Is it safe to travel in Northern Pakistan?

The Northern Areas of Pakistan are generally safe for tourists. Our team prioritizes safety and stays updated on regional conditions. We only operate tours in areas deemed safe for travel, and all our guides are highly experienced with local knowledge.
