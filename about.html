About Spangtik Adventure

Our Story

Passionate About Adventure Since 2010

Founded by a team of passionate mountaineers and trekking enthusiasts, Spangtik Adventure has been pioneering adventure tourism in the Northern Areas of Pakistan for over 13 years.

Named after the majestic Spangtik Peak in the Karakoram Range, our company was born from a deep love for the mountains and a desire to share the incredible natural beauty of Pakistan with the world. What began as a small operation with just three guides has grown into one of the region's most respected adventure travel companies.

Our mission has remained unchanged since day one: to provide safe, sustainable, and unforgettable trekking and adventure experiences while promoting cultural understanding and environmental conservation.

Muhammad Ali Khan

Founder & CEO
